About Us

Empowering Citizens with Information on Government Schemes

Our mission is to ensure that every citizen is informed about the government schemes they are eligible for, and to provide them with the necessary tools and guidance to apply for these schemes effectively.

Project Overview

Our project aims to notify users about new government schemes, check eligibility criteria, provide detailed information and required documents, and offer step-by-step guidance and video tutorials for applying.

Key Features

  • Real-time notifications of new schemes
  • Personalized eligibility checks
  • Comprehensive documentation and guidance
  • User-friendly interface
